Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 June 2026

ई-वाणी (E-Vani) ("we", "us", "the Site") is a free digital wisdom tool created and maintained by SiteLab India, Talwandi, Kota, Rajasthan. This Privacy Policy explains, in plain language, what happens — and what does not happen — to your information when you use this Site.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not ask you to create an account, sign up, or provide your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal detail to use E-Vani. There is no login, no registration form, and no field anywhere on this Site that asks for personal information.

2. Information Stored on Your Device

When you tap "Yes / Somewhat / Not really" after reading a shloka, that response is saved only inside your own browser's local storage — a small data file kept on your own device. This information:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is never sent to any server we control
  • Is never seen by us in any form
  • Can be erased at any time by clearing your browser's site data

3. Hosting and Technical Logs

This Site is hosted on GitHub Pages, a service operated by GitHub, Inc. Like any website host, GitHub may automatically record standard technical information — such as IP address, browser type, and access times — in its own server logs, in accordance with GitHub's own Privacy Statement. We do not have access to these logs and do not use them for any purpose.

4. Fonts Loaded from Google

This Site uses Google Fonts (Noto Sans Devanagari) so that Sanskrit text displays correctly on your screen. When your browser loads this font, a small request is sent to Google's servers, which may log your IP address as described in Google's own Privacy Policy. We do not control this process and receive no data from it.

5. No Advertising, No Tracking, No Marketing Cookies

E-Vani does not run advertising, does not use tracking pixels, and does not set any cookies of its own for analytics or marketing purposes.

6. Sharing Your Guidance

If you tap "Share," your device's own native sharing menu opens, using your browser or operating system's built-in share function. We do not see, log, or store what you share, or with whom you share it.

7. Children's Privacy

This Site does not knowingly collect personal information from any visitor, including children, because it does not collect personal information from anyone at all.

8. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes in the future — for example, if optional newsletter sign-up or analytics is added — we will update this page and revise the "Last updated" date above. We encourage you to review this page occasionally.

9. Contact Us

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to SiteLab India via sitelabindia.in.
