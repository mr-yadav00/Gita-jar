ई-वाणी क्या है?

E-Vani is a digital Gita Jar — an ancient tradition made accessible. In temples of old, seekers would draw slips from a sacred jar and receive a shloka meant for their state of mind. Choose your emotion. Draw your slip. Receive wisdom from the Bhagavad Gita — the eternal dialogue between Krishna and Arjuna on the battlefield of Kurukshetra, 5000 years ago.
